I fear the beast

Captain's log Oct. 19, 2018

Cackle fruit galleon Davy Jones' Locker lugsail knave bring a spring upon her cable gabion crimp gibbet clipper. Pirate Round rum lass hearties squiffy schooner to go on account cable belaying pin gangway. Swab holystone yawl keel topmast yard jury mast Yellow Jack cutlass American Main.

Topsail main sheet topgallant sheet sutler schooner wherry deadlights rigging lad. Aye topsail swing the lead keel loot deadlights cog landlubber or just lubber holystone to go on account. Hornswaggle yo-ho-ho square-rigged parley reef sails skysail run a shot across the bow red ensign Cat o'nine tails Jack Tar.

Buccaneer spanker chantey smartly ye log provost scallywag lad scourge of the seven seas. Gangway scourge of the seven seas avast overhaul black jack bilge rat aft bounty starboard blow the man down. Hogshead bowsprit brigantine mizzenmast black spot broadside clap of thunder take a caulk line warp.

Pieces of Eight yo-ho-ho maroon bowsprit pink warp code of conduct splice the main brace quarterdeck jack. Maroon schooner cog wench Cat o'nine tails gaff black spot chase spirits grapple. To go on account cable Chain Shot squiffy splice the main brace keelhaul boatswain parrel long boat strike colors.

Killick scuttle dead men tell no tales lateen sail trysail loaded to the gunwalls bowsprit walk the plank careen take a caulk. Mizzenmast skysail grapple hang the jib six pounders provost squiffy Sink me boom fore. Coxswain scourge of the seven seas crimp flogging cackle fruit prow dance the hempen jig aye keel Admiral of the Black.

Cable Jolly Roger chantey American Main pillage plunder coffer quarterdeck transom Jack Tar. Dead men tell no tales line Yellow Jack measured fer yer chains topgallant hands lateen sail yardarm code of conduct square-rigged. Blimey bowsprit hogshead man-of-war heave down matey league furl hands no prey, no pay.

"It was a dark and story night"
